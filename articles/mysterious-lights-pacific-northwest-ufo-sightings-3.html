Mysterious Lights Sighted Over Pacific Northwest Spark UFO Speculations

Several witnesses reported seeing unexplained lights hovering over the Pacific Northwest, stirring discussions about possible UFO activity. The origin and nature of these lights remain uncertain as investigations continue.
